what is the scope of this platform?

An anonymous helpdesk is a platform or system designed to facilitate communication between users seeking assistance or support and those providing it, while maintaining the anonymity of the users seeking help.

what is its objectives?

  • Anonymous Problem Submission
  • Facilitating Anonymous Communication
  • Addressing Stigma and Barriers to Help-seeking
  • Promoting Mental Health and Well-being

How does our platform prioritize user privacy and anonymity?

  • users create accounts with anonymous usernames.
  • Users are not required to provide real names, email addresses, or phone numbers during registration.
  • empowers individuals to engage with the helpdesk system while preserving their privacy.

What environment does this approach foster for users?

a safe and confidential environment for users to seek assistance, share concerns, and access support resources without fear of their identity being compromised.

Who benefits from the inclusivity of our helpdesk platform?

The inclusivity of our helpdesk platform benefits users from various backgrounds, including the general public, employees, students, and any user group requiring support.

How do problem commenters contribute to the platform's community?

Problem commenters contribute to the platform's community by offering support, empathy, and helpful advice to users who have shared their problems.
